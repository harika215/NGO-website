About us


                  HOPE is a Non-Governmental Organisation(NGO) that supports underprivileged children in India. We, in Hope, collectively help the children in need. We rescue children from child labor, sexual abuse, violence, child trafficking, addiction, runaways, severe health conditions, child marriage, and educational purposes, etc. We assure the brighter future of our kids and ensure that they reach great heights in their careers.

NGOs, sometimes called civil societies, are organized on community, national and international levels to serve a social or political goal such as humanitarian causes or the environment.

Our Beginning


                 As our logo suggests, we aim to lend hand to the needy and poor. We concrate mostly on children but we aspire to help everyone in need. We started as a group of ten aspirants who always dreamt of helping other people. Now we have grown into a huge community of ten thousand donors. We are glad that we have reached this point with your constant love & support. We hope to grow more, donate more and help even more children with the blessings of the lord.
